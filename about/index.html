Learn About Our Club

Who We Are

  • We are a student run organization at Flagler Palm Coast High School.

    We are full of kind and welcoming members and officers who all share the same love for web development.

What We Do

  • We teach students the basics of web development and how they can use these skills.

    Each member can work on their own personal website, or work collectively as a group on the club's website.

  • We also take on community submissions for websites and help turn their idea into a reality.

    This is not for a profit, but is instead community service, which can be put on resumes and applications.

When We Meet

  • We hold meetings every Tuesday after school (excluding days off of school) from 2:25 to 4:00.

    Meetings are held in Mrs. Castañeda's room, 2-110.
